**Customer Maturity Model (Divergent Academy / XA)**

Three-step framework converting successful customer behaviors into a systematic retention and expansion engine.

**Step 1 — ID maturity tiers:** Define progressively advanced use cases that correlate with retention. Start from known successful customers or best hypothesis.

**Step 2 — Define each level** across three vectors:
- *Behavior*: leading customer actions signaling current maturity level
- *Customer Value Metric*: quantifiable outcome measuring the behavior
- *Features*: product functionality within your solution driving the behavior

**Step 3 — Action plays:** Build adoption playbooks that guide all customers toward deeper maturity levels. Each playbook targets the behavior gap between consecutive tiers.

**Purpose:** Converts passive product adoption into structured, measurable progression — compounding retention depth across cohort lifetime.
