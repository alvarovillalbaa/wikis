# Sales Learning And GTM Map

[Source: Alvaro's Brain]

Sales learning should combine founder-led sales, first sales hires, enterprise selling, competitor handling, proof-of-work personalization, validation depth, likeability/trust, and repeatable country expansion.

## Compiled Truth
- Early sales needs founder proximity: talk to users, prove effort, and learn the sales motion before delegation.
- Hiring first reps requires explicit screening for learning speed, resilience, founder-market understanding, and repeatability.
- Enterprise GTM depends on stakeholder navigation, trust, competitor framing, and multi-level validation.
- Competitor conversations should surface buyer criteria and create positioning clarity, not become feature-comparison theater.
- A pitch needs layered validation: problem, consequence, and buyer belief.
- AI-assisted sales sources should be treated as tactics until verified against real pipeline outcomes.
