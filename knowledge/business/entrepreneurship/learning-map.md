# Entrepreneurship Learning Map

[Source: Alvaro's Brain]

Entrepreneurship learning should cover non-scalable early motion, venture creation, leadership, innovation, team building, product development, marketing, strategy, negotiation, and advanced strategy.

## Compiled Source Clusters
- Early-stage execution: Paul Graham's "do things that do not scale" video source.
- Venture formation: MIT New Enterprises.
- Leadership/team: MIT Leadership Development and Building and Leading Effective Teams.
- Innovation/product: MIT Managing Innovation and Entrepreneurship; Product Design and Development.
- Go-to-market: MIT Entrepreneurial Marketing.
- Strategy/negotiation: MIT Technology Strategy, Negotiation and Conflict Management, Advanced Strategy.
