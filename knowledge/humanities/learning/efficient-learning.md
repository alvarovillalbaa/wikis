**Organic Learning:** identifying a "what" (e.g., everything surrounding us was designed by someone) transforms ambient experience into continuous cross-domain input.

**Three structural effects:**
- *Selective retention pressure* — only lessons with durable cross-domain applicability worth encoding; related to Compounded Learning (sales ↔ product ↔ engineering transfer)
- *Analytical saturation* — hyper-analysis defaults to critique; correct decisions are equally instructive as failures — balance is required
- *Hierarchical abstraction imperative* — high-specificity folders (SaaS design, product design) must collapse into high-abstraction folders (design) to maximize reuse surface and reduce cognitive load
